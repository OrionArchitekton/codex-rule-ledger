# Visual Thesis

## Visual thesis

Codex Rule Ledger should feel like a forensic editorial desk: warm paper,
precise ink, stamped evidence states, and a single vermilion action accent—not a
generic dark AI dashboard.

## Content plan

The first viewport is the audit workspace: compact product masthead, launch
capture and reconstructed chain at left, obligation ledger in the center, and a
source/evidence inspector at right. Provenance is always visible. A short lower
section explains the four result semantics and the product's limits; export and
live-analysis actions remain secondary to reading the evidence.

## Interaction plan

- Reconstructed instruction sources enter in merge order with a restrained
  stagger so precedence is visually legible.
- Selecting a ledger row draws a rule-to-evidence line and slides the inspector
  into focus without navigating away.
- Status filters behave like editorial index tabs; hover and focus reveal the
  exact semantics rather than decorative motion.

## Design tokens

- Display face: Fraunces.
- Technical/body face: IBM Plex Mono.
- Paper: warm ivory.
- Ink: near-black blue.
- Action accent: vermilion.
- Evidence states: pine green, signal red, ochre, and slate.
- Corners stay nearly square; shadows are reserved for the active evidence
  sheet only.
